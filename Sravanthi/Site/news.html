What's on the board!

Sravanthi Cinemass has acquired the RAJA VIJAYA SIMHA cinema rights for Telugu language.

RAJA VIJAYA SIMHA is a fantasy action film written & directed by debutante Director Sakthivel Perumalsamy. It stars Bobby Simha, Kalaiyarasan & Reshmi Menon. Produced by Sravanthi Cinemass, the film has Cinematography by Ravindranath Guru and Music composed by Achu Rajamani. RAJA VIJAYA SIMHA is a Ultimate Revenge saga spanning across three generations. The film also uses Motion capture technology in a five minute opening sequence.

Cast

Bobby Simha, Kalaiyarasan, Reshmi Menon, Charle, Manobala, Appukutty, Kaali Venkat, R. S. Shivaji, Guru Lukman, Karthik Yogi, Sandra Amy, Gajaraj, Oviyar Veera Santhanam, Anthony Daasan, B. H. Tharun Kumar.
